About

QuarkX founder Koay Xian Jing operates purely at the intersection of deep scientific research and industrial systems engineering.

His technical foundation was forged at Cambridge through the application of deep learning to massive environmental datasets. His research—mapping over 100,000 tropical forest canopies via Mask R-CNN—was published in top-tier journals and established his baseline for high-fidelity physical modeling.

That academic rigor met enterprise reality at Huawei Technologies. There, he developed DPU hardware offloading architectures for mission-critical enterprise storage, fundamentally doubling Storage Area Network IOPS and slashing latency across the stack.

As Lead Engineer at Ant International, he scaled this discipline. He architected organization-wide agentic workflows, designing autonomous LLM frameworks that wiped out thousands of hours of manual operational overhead and earned CTO-level deployment approval.

Today, QuarkX channels this exact profile—scientific depth and bare-metal engineering discipline—into autonomous systems for the physical world. We don't build temporary prototypes; we build resilient systems that drive measurable operational outcomes.

Good Questions

What does QuarkX actually do?

We build autonomous software explicitly designed to close the loop. Traditional AI stops at a dashboard recommendation; our systems observe the environment, reason about the state, and execute the intervention autonomously without waiting for human authorization.

What is an "agentic AI" system?

It's the paradigm shift from copilot to autopilot. Agentic AI involves specialized models equipped with dynamic tool use, memory architectures, and operational agency to complete complex workflows. It is the difference between a weather forecast and an autonomous climate control grid.

Can you share who your clients are?

We engineer systems for enterprises operating critical infrastructure across the telecommunications, energy, and environmental sectors. We default to strict NDAs. If you have an operational bottleneck, we are happy to discuss our technical capabilities confidentially.

What makes QuarkX different?

The rare overlap of disciplines. We bring Cambridge computational modeling, low-level firmware optimization from Huawei, and production-scale agent orchestration from Ant International. We aren't a generative AI wrapper agency; we are deep systems engineers.
